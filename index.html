Our Story

Family-owned and community-focused since 1995

A Mountain Tradition

Nestled in the heart of the mountains, Buffalo Mountain Grille has been serving authentic, locally-sourced cuisine for over 25 years. Our commitment to quality ingredients and warm hospitality has made us a beloved destination for both locals and visitors alike.

Every dish tells a story of our mountain heritage, from our signature bison steaks to our handcrafted desserts. We believe in supporting local farmers and producers, ensuring that every meal is not just delicious, but also sustainable and community-driven.
